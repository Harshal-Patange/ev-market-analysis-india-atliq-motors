## 📌 Problem Statement

**AtliQ Motors** is an automotive giant from the USA specializing in electric vehicles. In the last 5 years, their market share rose to 25% in the electric and hybrid vehicles segment in North America. As part of their expansion plans, they want to launch their bestselling models in India where their market share is less than 2%. Bruce Haryali, chief of AtliQ Motors India, tasked the data analytics team with a detailed market study before proceeding.

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview

> Data source: **Vahan Sewa** (Government of India) — monthly EV sales data from FY 2022 to FY 2024.

| Dataset | Key Columns |
|---|---|
| `electric_vehicle_sales_by_state.csv` | date, state, vehicle_category, electric_vehicles_sold, total_vehicles_sold |
| `electric_vehicle_sales_by_makers.csv` | date, vehicle_category, maker, electric_vehicles_sold |
| `dim_date.csv` | date, fiscal_year, quarter |

**Key metrics used:**
- **Penetration Rate** = (Electric Vehicles Sold / Total Vehicles Sold) × 100
- **CAGR** = [(Ending Value / Beginning Value)^(1/n)] − 1
- Fiscal year: April 1 to March 31

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Market leader:** OLA Electric dominates 2-wheeler EV sales in FY 2023 and FY 2024
- **Top penetration states (2W):** Ladakh (26.92%), Goa (24.01%), Kerala (16.35%)
- **Top penetration states (4W):** Kerala (8.72%), Chandigarh (7.17%), Goa (5.28%)
- **Best manufacturing state:** Maharashtra — strong industrial base, high EV demand, government incentives
- **Recommended brand ambassador:** Rohit Sharma — mass appeal, trustworthy image, family audience
- **Growth signal:** EV market share is rising even as overall vehicle revenue declines

---

## 🏆 Strategic Recommendations

1. **Focus on high-growth states** — Target Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu for faster market entry
2. **Invest in charging infrastructure** — Expand stations in cities and highways to reduce range anxiety
3. **Build brand awareness** — Run campaigns on cost savings and sustainability with a strong brand ambassador
