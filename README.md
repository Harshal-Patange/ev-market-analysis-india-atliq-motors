## 📌 Project Overview

AtliQ Motors currently holds less than 2% market share in India. This project performs a deep-dive analysis across 28+ states, 20+ EV manufacturers, and 3 fiscal years to uncover growth opportunities, market leaders, and strategic entry points.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Data coverage | FY 2022 – FY 2024 |
| States analyzed | 28+ |
| EV makers covered | 20+ |
| Primary questions answered | 10 |

| `Codebasics.pptx` | 23-slide presentation — market insights, secondary research, strategic recommendations |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Market leader:** OLA Electric dominates 2-Wheeler EV sales in both FY 2023 and FY 2024
- **Top penetration states (2W):** Ladakh (26.92%), Goa (24.01%), Kerala (16.35%)
- **Top penetration states (4W):** Kerala (8.72%), Chandigarh (7.17%), Goa (5.28%)
- **Best manufacturing state:** Maharashtra — strong industrial base, high EV demand, government incentives
- **Recommended brand ambassador:** Rohit Sharma — mass appeal, trustworthy image, family audience connect
- **Growth signal:** EV market share is rising even as overall vehicle revenue declines

---

## 📊 Primary Analysis (Excel — Q1 to Q10)

1. Top & bottom EV 2-wheeler makers by sales (FY 2023 & FY 2024)
2. Top 5 states by EV penetration rate for 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers (FY 2024)
3. States with declining EV penetration (FY 2022–2024)
4. Quarterly sales trends for top 5 EV 4-wheeler makers (FY 2022–2024)
5. Delhi vs Karnataka — EV sales and penetration comparison (FY 2024)
6. CAGR of top EV makers (FY 2022–2024)
7. Top 10 states by CAGR in total vehicle sales
8. Peak and low EV sales seasons/months
9. Projected EV sales for top 10 states by 2030
10. Revenue growth rate estimate (2W and 4W EVs, 2022 vs 2024)

---

## 🏆 Strategic Recommendations

1. **Focus on high-growth states** — Target Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu for faster market entry and sales growth
2. **Invest in charging infrastructure** — Expand charging stations in cities and on highways to reduce range anxiety
3. **Build brand awareness** — Run campaigns highlighting cost savings and sustainability with a strong brand ambassador
